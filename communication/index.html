Communication

Communication and graphics cards are related on multiple levels. Communication with others in the modern-day usually relates to computers in one way or another. If you want to text a friend, look at the news online, watch a video, or play a game, we rely on the ability to see what is happening on the screen. Without GPU's nothing would show up on our screens, and all the things previously stated would not be able to exist.
