Manufacturing

Graphics cards are made with serveral materials from all around the world including silicon, tantalum, palladium, copper, boron, cobalt, tungsten, and several chemicals. All of these materials then need to be processed and turned into several components of a graphics card. Then all of the components go together to make the graphics card. Finally the graphics cards are tested.
